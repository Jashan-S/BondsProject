The bond market charges you for being invisible.

Corporate bonds don't trade on an exchange with one public price. They trade dealer-to-dealer, and the price you're shown depends on who's asking. Same bond, same day, two very different fills:

Institutional print · $2,000,000 block

$98.40 YTM 5.42%

What the trade tape (TRACE) shows professionals paid.

Retail fill · $25,000 order

$99.65 YTM 5.18%

Roughly 125 bps of markup, silently embedded in the price.

Illustrative example. The markup never appears as a fee — it's just a worse price. Basispoint's fair-value check compares any quote you're shown against recent prints on the tape, so the spread stops being invisible.
